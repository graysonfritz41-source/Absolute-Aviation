Training Timeline

Your Journey to the Airlines

Our structured curriculum takes you through each certification step-by-step, building skills progressively over 10-12 months.

01

Private Pilot Certificate

Months 1-3

Begin your aviation journey with fundamental flying skills. Learn aircraft systems, navigation, weather, and flight maneuvers. Complete your first solo flight and build confidence in the cockpit.

02

Instrument Rating

Months 4-6

Master instrument flight rules and flying in low visibility conditions. Develop precision flying skills using advanced avionics and navigation systems in our G1000-equipped aircraft.

03

Commercial Pilot Certificate

Months 7-8

Refine your skills to professional standards. Learn advanced maneuvers, commercial operations, and the business of aviation. Earn your ticket to fly for compensation.

04

Multi-Engine Rating

Month 9

Transition to multi-engine aircraft in our Piper Seminoles. Learn complex aircraft systems, engine-out procedures, and multi-engine operations essential for airline flying.

05

Certified Flight Instructor

Months 10-12

Learn to teach and build your hours. Develop communication and instructional skills while gaining the experience needed for airline applications. Many of our CFIs are hired internally!
